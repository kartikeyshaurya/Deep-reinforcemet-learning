Bandung in Fashion

Bandung in Fashion is one of the database system program used to collect all about the fashion industry in Bandung City, established since 2018 with the idea of Muhammad Rizal Fahmi is one of the industrial engineering students Pasundan University that sparked the final project of developing the fashion industry in the city of Bandung.

This website aims to unify all aspects of the creative economy ecosystem that has been triggered before. This program is made for the promotion of the entire fashion industry in Bandung. In it will be explained about the various categories of fashion in Bandung, a famous area that sells fashion products, discussion forums, transactions and galleries and the closest activities on fashion in the city of Bandung.
